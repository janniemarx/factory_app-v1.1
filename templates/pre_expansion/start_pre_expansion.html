Pre-Expansion Start Checklist
  • 1. Check if all material in expansion chamber and in fluidized bed drier are cleared of lumps and other objects, and check agitating rods.
  • 2. Check air filter and oil lubricator. Remove water in filter and add Viscosity Grade 10 oil in lubricator if oil level is low.
  • 3. Before opening main steam valve, open drain valve to let water in main steam pipe out. Close drain valve.
  • 4. Check pressure gauge, temperature gauge, current gauge, volt gauge and motors. Please report and repair any malfunction immediately.
  • 5. Check electronic scale, if it's not precise, arrange for calibration.
  • 6. Check earth Connection.
  • 7. Check sealing of 80mm Butterfly valve on filling port and vent port. Replace seal immediately if leakage is detected.
  • 8. Check all valves and pipes. Any leaks should be repaired immediately.
  • 9. Manually test and check safety valve.
  • 10. Pressure of expansion chamber is controlled by a pressure gauge switch, connection between the pressure gauge switch and the chamber is a copper pipe. Make sure Copper pipe is not blocked.
Please complete all checklist items before starting.
