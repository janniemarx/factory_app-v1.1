Block Production Start Checklist
  • Check for any unusual sounds, vibrations, or odors.
  • Inspect the machine for signs of leaks, such as steam, hydraulic fluid, or water.
  • Complete overall visual inspection to be sure all equipment is operating and safety systems are in place.
  • Check the steam supply pressure and ensure it's within the recommended range.
  • Inspect steam valves, pipes, and fittings for leaks or corrosion.
  • Verify that the steam traps are functioning properly.
  • Check steam hoses and connections for wear or damage.
  • Check hydraulic fluid levels and top up if needed.
  • Inspect hydraulic hoses, fittings, and connections for leaks or damage.
  • Operate hydraulic functions (if applicable) to ensure proper response.
  • Inspect cooling fans, radiators, and cooling mechanisms for debris or blockages.
  • Verify that the cooling system is effectively dissipating heat.
  • Inspect electrical cables, connections, and wiring for wear or damage.
  • Check the control panel for any error codes or abnormalities.
  • Test emergency stop buttons and safety interlocks.
  • Ensure that all safety guards, shields, and covers are properly in place.
  • Test emergency stop buttons and safety switches for functionality.
  • Verify that safety signage is clear and visible.
  • Apply appropriate lubrication to moving parts as recommended by the manufacturer.
  • Record maintenance tasks, observations, and any issues in a maintenance logbook.
  • Keep track of steam pressure readings, fluid levels, and any adjustments made.
  • Keep the machine and its surrounding area clean and free from debris.
Please complete all checklist items before starting the block production session.
